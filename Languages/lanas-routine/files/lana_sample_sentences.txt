Asuu pattirito enö Lana. = Lana lives in a big city.
Aloaa murimäiky teritöen? = What is her favourite time of day?
Laitaa nata räinä ni pertoo lykäen etan onkonoto nopo tylei. = When she opens her front door and walks her dog every morning at half past six.
Pertoo lykäen intala tynnä erto. = She usually walks her dog for thirty minutes.
Opinkkaa ommuro en aja. = Then she drives to work.
Puukii opinkka ommuro tynnä aitupana. = It takes her forty-five minutes to drive to work.
Ätyy aommu Lana? = What does Lara do for work?
Talluu mertia Lana. = Lana owns a shop.
Punnee mertiato en menti paatiroto nopo tylei. = Every morning she arrives at the shop at quarter to eight.
